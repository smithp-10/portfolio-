Market Microstructure
Order flow, bid-ask dynamics, and price discovery mechanisms. Fascinated by how limit order books reveal the hidden equilibrium between informed and uninformed traders at the millisecond level.
Stochastic Calculus
It’s Calculus — Itô’s lemma, Girsanov’s theorem, risk-neutral measures. The mathematical language of uncertainty translated into pricing and hedging. Beauty lives in the rigour.
Statistical Arbitrage
Pairs trading, cointegration, mean-reversion strategies. Finding the edges that persist across time by modelling relationships that the market hasn’t yet fully priced in. Signal extraction from noise.
Behavioural Finance
Kahneman, Thaler, Shiller — the cognitive architecture of market inefficiency. Systematic biases as predictable alpha sources. Understanding why markets deviate from rationality is itself an edge.
Quantitative Research
Reads more quant papers than anything else. Empirical asset pricing, factor models, volatility forecasting — where academic rigour meets the harsh feedback loop of live capital. The best papers bleed money if applied naively.
Risk & Portfolio Theory
Kelly criterion, Markowitz optimisation, factor decomposition. Risk is not a constraint to minimise — it’s the raw material of return. Sizing and portfolio construction is where quant thinking compounds most powerfully.
